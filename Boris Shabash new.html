What Can I Do For You?

Boris Shabash, PhD

I received my PhD in Bioinformatics from Simon Fraser University in 2018 and have since dedicated myself to learning data science. I am passionate about deriving insights from data, particularly using the techniques of machine learning, probabilistic programming, text analysis, and big data. I also have extensive experience working with data visualization techniques and tools.



With 17 years in programming experience ranging from C++, Java, SQL, and Python, I have the techniques necessary to sink my teeth and solve any problem with the best tools.
I have worked on a variety of projects ranging from bioinformatics to finance, and have presented my work in a variety of conferences around the world (in The Netherlands, Japan, and United States to name a few), so I am no stranger to researching problem spaces, solution architecture, and presentation of my work.



Currently I am a Lead Educator and Team Lead at BrainStation. Guiding the future minds of data scientists and preparing them to meet the challenges tomorrow will bring to the field.
